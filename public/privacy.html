Privacy Policy

Effective May 17, 2026 · Last updated May 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Peter Jones Experience Design LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Hona iOS application and the website at hona.fitness (together, the “Service”).

The short version. Hona is designed to keep your training data with you. Health and training information you bring into Hona is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly choose a feature that requires it to leave (notably, the AI coaching insight). We don’t sell your data. We don’t share it with advertisers. We don’t use it to train AI models.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide

  • Email address — when you join the beta waitlist on hona.fitness.
  • Manual entries in the app — InBody body-composition scans, recovery notes, and similar logs you type in.
  • Files you import — Cronometer CSV exports, if you choose to use the nutrition feature.

1.2 Information you authorize from your device

  • Apple HealthKit data — heart rate variability (HRV), resting heart rate, sleep, respiratory rate, body weight, and similar metrics — only the categories you grant permission to in iOS.
  • Apple Calendar events (EventKit) — workouts pulled from a calendar you connect, typically a TrainingPeaks-synced calendar.

1.3 Information collected automatically

  • Crash and diagnostic data — if you opt in to share with Apple, standard iOS crash reports may be shared with us via Apple’s tooling. No personally-identifying training content is included.
  • Website analytics — we currently do not run analytics on hona.fitness.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate the Service and present your training, nutrition, body-composition, and recovery information back to you.
  • To generate the AI coaching insight when you tap the button to request one (see Section 4).
  • To send you the TestFlight beta invitation and occasional build notes, if you signed up for the waitlist.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve the Service.

We do not use your training, health, or body-composition data to train machine-learning models, sell it to third parties, or share it with advertisers.

3. Where Your Data Lives

Most data Hona uses stays on your iPhone in the app’s local storage and in Apple HealthKit. We do not run a server that stores your training history, biometrics, or body-composition data.

Exceptions:

  • Email addresses entered into the beta waitlist form on hona.fitness are not currently transmitted to or stored by any third-party service. The waitlist form is a placeholder during early development and will be wired up to a mailing-list provider before public launch; this policy will be updated at that time.
  • AI coaching insight requests are processed by Anthropic — see Section 4.

4. AI Coaching Insight (Anthropic API)

The “Generate coaching insight” button in the Body Metrics widget sends a small payload of your recent training and body-metrics summary to Anthropic, the maker of Claude, for processing. The response is returned to your device and displayed in the app.

What is sent:

  • Numeric summaries of the metrics shown in the widget (HRV, resting heart rate, sleep duration, respiratory rate, recent training-load numbers).
  • Brief contextual labels (training phase, weeks-to-race) so the response is relevant.

What is not sent:

  • Your name, email, location, or any direct identifier.
  • Raw HealthKit time-series data.
  • Nutrition logs, recovery notes, or InBody scan history.

Anthropic processes the request under its own Privacy Policy. Anthropic does not use API content to train its models (per Anthropic’s commercial terms in effect at the time of this writing — verify the current policy).

6. Cookies and Tracking

The website at hona.fitness does not set tracking cookies and does not use any web analytics service. If this changes in the future, this section will be updated before any tracking is deployed. The iOS application does not use third-party SDKs for analytics or advertising.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 16. If you believe we have collected information from a child, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Your Choices and Rights

  • Revoke HealthKit / Calendar access at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.
  • Delete the app to remove all locally-stored Hona data from your device.
  • Unsubscribe from build emails using the unsubscribe link in any email we send you.
  • Access, correct, or delete any other information we hold about you — contact us using the address below.

If you are in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you have additional rights under the GDPR or CCPA, including the right to portability and the right to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

9. Data Security

We use commercially reasonable measures to protect information processed by the Service. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee that information will never be accessed or disclosed in ways that contravene this Policy.

10. International Transfers

The Service is operated from the United States. If you use the Service from outside the US, information processed by the Service may be transferred to the US, where data-protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We’ll update the “Effective” date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the app or via email. Continued use of the Service after a change means you accept the revised Policy.
